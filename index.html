ABOUT US

Brown Bear tailor & co was founded with only 1 thing in mind. To bring the luxury of the finest bespoke to your doorstep.

Bespoke is an art which over the years has been limited to very formal or Official work wear & a lot many do not experience this satisfying process of getting measured & wearing a garment that meant for you alone.

The idea behind Brown Bear is to take this art to everyone & create this experience for all thus we have created an entire line of Casual clothing which is fit for all, weather you work from home of office.

We Create anything from a suit to a topcoat or a shirt Denims and polos, as we have a wide range of products for you to select from.

Brown Bear tailor company is an initiative to achieve sustainable luxury fashion through our responsible choice of materials &. Efforts to create a healthier future for our Planet. With no compromise on quality & craftmanship.
